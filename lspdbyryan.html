Attaching Bodycam

Attaching Bodycam (Uniform)

/me Takes out bodycam, attaches it to chest, checks its ballistic, water proof
/me makes sure it is recording and checks for the red light
/do It is recording, is ballistic and water proof
/me connects PDA to the nearest cell tower

While Undercover

/me takes out bodycam and attaches it to belt, hides it, checks its ballistic and water proof
/me makes sure it is recording and checks for the red light
/do It is recording, is ballistic and water proof
/me connects PDA to the nearest cell tower
🔄

Refreshing Bodycam

Refreshing Bodycam

/me refreshing bodycam
/do It is recording
💾

Saving Bodycam

Saving Bodycam

/me saves bodycam, uploads it to the LSPD Cloud and continues recording
/do It is recording
🚁

While Undercover

Breaking the Trunk

Breaking the Trunk

/me carefully inspects the trunk and uses a crowbar to attempt opening it
/me applies pressure with the crowbar to force the trunk open

Handing Footage to the Lawyer

Handing Footage to the Lawyer

/me saves bodycam contents onto an SD card and uploads contents to LSPD servers
/me Takes SD card out of the bodycam and hands it to the lawyer
🏁

Finishing Your Shift

Finishing Your Shift

/me saves bodycam contents onto an SD card and uploads contents to LSPD servers
/me puts the badge in the locker
🔍

Searching a Trunk

Taking Evidence

When License Plate Not Visible of Car

/me feels the edges of the vehicle for VIN and checks for ownership

When License Plate Not Visible of Bike

/me checks the VIN from the steering neck of the bike and checks for ownership
Then press G on vehicle and view vehicle information.
